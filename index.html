Water: Every Drop Counts

Learn about water wastage and how you can help conserve this precious resource.

Water Wastage Facts

70%

of the Earth's surface is covered with water, but only 1% is accessible for human use.

3.4 million

people die each year from water-related diseases.

80%

of water used in agriculture is wasted due to inefficiency.

Impact of Water Wastage

Water wastage leads to water scarcity, which impacts ecosystems, food security, and human health worldwide.

Environmental Impact

Water scarcity affects habitats and biodiversity, leading to the depletion of rivers, lakes, and ecosystems.

Human Impact

Lack of access to clean water affects health, sanitation, and food supply in communities worldwide.

Solutions to Reduce Water Wastage

Fix Leaks

Repairing household leaks can save up to 10,000 gallons of water per year.

Efficient Irrigation

Using drip irrigation and watering plants during cool hours reduces water waste.

Water Conservation Education

Teaching communities about efficient water usage creates long-lasting awareness.
